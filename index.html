Mobile Car with Robot Arm

Check out this embedded systems project I worked on for EECS 373 (Embedded Systems) at UMich in 2018. I worked with a team of 3 people to build a car with a robotic arm on top. We hacked a PS2 controller in order to control it and set up a wireless connection using a zigbee. We used the ARM cortex M3 processor
